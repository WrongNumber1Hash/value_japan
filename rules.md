# **Value Japan**

*Using copilot or GenAI to evaluate public companies in Japan*
**1 Player | Ages 13+ | 20–40 Minutes**

Use with a simulated trading game to learn about investing and trading securities in Japan.

---

## **Objective**

To identify Japanese companies to invest in and understand stocks under 1,000 JPY each. The objective is to use with a simulated trading game to learn about trading and investing.

---

## **Components**

Google worksheet with links to ft.com and google for stock information and charts. This is for English content, but for Japanese content, we can use nikkei.com and Yahoo Japan.

---

## **Setup**

1. Set up a google app account or workspace account to create a spreadsheet. 
2. Go to https://www.jpx.co.jp to download a list of Japanese companies with fiscal years ending in March. 
3. First, go to https://www.jpx.co.jp/english/listing/index.html.
4. See earnings announcements https://www.jpx.co.jp/english/listing/event-schedules/financial-announcement/index.html.
5. There is only a Japanese language version available here https://www.jpx.co.jp/listing/event-schedules/shareholders-mtg/index.html.
6. For an English language list in csv format for upload to Google workspace or Google Drive, you can use the Japannext Alternative Exchange.
