# Code of Conduct: Emotional & Psychological Integrity

1. Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we as contributors and players pledge to making participation in our project and our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

2. Specialized Standards for Psychological Play

Because this game involves psychological interaction, we hold players to a "Safety First" standard:

    Respect Boundaries: While the game encourages emotional engagement, players must respect "Hard Stops." If a player indicates they are uncomfortable or distressed, all psychological "play" must cease immediately.

    No Malicious Manipulation: Using game mechanics to intentionally cause real-world psychological trauma, triggers, or distress is strictly prohibited.

    Consent is Continuous: Just because a player agreed to interact once does not mean they agree to all forms of interaction. Consent can be withdrawn at any time.

    Distinguish Character from Self: Maintain a clear boundary between in-game emotional roles and real-world personal attacks.

3. Prohibited Harassment

Examples of behavior that will not be tolerated include:

    The use of sexualized language or imagery and unwelcome sexual attention or advances.

    Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks.

    Public or private harassment.

    Publishing others' private information, such as a physical or electronic address, without explicit permission (Doxing).

    Gaslighting: Intentionally and maliciously distorting another player's perception of reality for non-gameplay purposes.

4. Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying the standards of acceptable behavior and are expected to take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any instances of unacceptable behavior.

5. Scope

This Code of Conduct applies both within project spaces (the game itself, GitHub Issues, PRs) and in public spaces when an individual is representing the project or its community.

# 心理的プレイに関する特別基準 (Specialized Standards)

本ゲームは心理的な相互作用を伴うため、プレイヤーには「セーフティ・ファースト（安全第一）」の基準を守ることが求められます。

境界線の尊重: 
    ゲームは感情的な関わりを促しますが、プレイヤーは他者の「ハード・ストップ（拒絶の意思表示）」を尊重しなければなりません。他者が不快感や苦痛を示した場合、すべての心理的な「プレイ」は直ちに中止されるべきです。

悪意ある操作の禁止: 
    ゲームのメカニズムを、意図的に現実世界の心理的トラウマや苦痛を引き起こすために使用することは固く禁じられています。

継続的な同意: 
    一度の対話に同意したからといって、すべての形態の対話に同意したことにはなりません。同意はいつでも撤回することができます。

キャラクターと自己の分離: 
    ゲーム内の感情的な役割と、現実世界の個人攻撃を明確に区別してください。
